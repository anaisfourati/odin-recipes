Smoothie

Description

Whizz up a quick and filling peanut butter smoothie, with rolled oats and banana. Brilliant for breakfast, you can freeze the banana for extra thickness

Ingredients

  • 200ml oat milk
  • 1 banana
  • 20g peanut butter
  • 1tbsp rolled oats
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • pinch of allspice
  • pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Whizz all the ingredients together in a blender (one suitable for crushing ice) until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and serve.
  2. Enjoy!
